## Welcome to the Crate

**Get Da Choppah** is a browser-based sample chopper designed for one thing: helping scratch DJs build scratch tracks and sample sentences, fast.

No DAW bloat. No 47-step workflow. No subscription. Just:

1. **Source** — paste a YouTube/SoundCloud link, search by name, or upload your own file
2. **Chop** — drag across the waveform bar to slice regions
3. **Order** — drag your chops into the right sequence
4. **Export** — one file, ready to load into Serato, burn to wax, or throw on the SP

That's it. That's the whole philosophy. **Get in, chop, get out.**

## Who Is This For?

- **Scratch DJs** building scratch sentences and practice tracks
- **Battle DJs** prepping custom sample tracks
- **Turntablists** who need quick chops from any source
- **Beat diggers** extracting one-shots and breaks
- **Anyone** who's tired of opening a full DAW just to cut 8 samples

## Features at a Glance

| Feature | What It Means For You |
|---------|----------------------|
| YouTube + SoundCloud support | Dig through the internet's biggest crates |
| Local file upload | WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG — bring your own records |
| Visual drag-to-chop | See what you're cutting on the stream bar |
| Precision overlay | Zoom in and fine-tune with draggable handles or exact timestamps |
| Per-region colors | Never lose track of which chop is which |
| Overlap prevention | Clean cuts, no bleed-through |
| Undo / Redo | `Ctrl+Z` / `Ctrl+Y` — chop fearlessly |
| Drag-to-reorder queue | Set the exact sequence for your scratch track |
| Ready gating | Only export the chops you've approved |
| WAV / MP3 / FLAC export | One file with your chops in order, 1-second gaps between each |
